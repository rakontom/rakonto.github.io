Music. Sound. Noise

Kyiv native Max Rakonto makes electronic music for tender moments and noisy minds. Listen now on Bandcamp, SoundCloud, and YouTube.

Contact: play@rakon.to

To get a deeper dive, follow @sonic_flux on Telegram, Instagram, or subscribe to the newsletter.

In Sonic flux, Max explores music and ideas that fuel his sonic journey, both as a listener and as a music maker. It’s a mix of music write-ups, photos, videos, free-form essays, and notes about Rakonto’s releases.
