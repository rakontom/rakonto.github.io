Sonic flux

Sonic ﬂux is a pet project of Kyiv-based music maker Max Rakonto. It’s a one-person media merging music reviews, random excerpts, free-form essays, and sidenotes about Rakonto’s releases. Max explores music (and ideas behind it) that fuel his sonic journey both as a listener and a music maker. He writes in Russian, Ukrainian, and English.
